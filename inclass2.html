This website will help you to convert the temperature from Celsius to Fahrenheit and vice versa. Here I have created two buttons. One with name "Celsius to Fahrenheit" and other with name "Fahrenheit to Celsius".

If you click at the button named "Celsius to Fahrenheit", it will ask you to enter the temperature in Celsius and then it will convert it to Fahrenheit and then will display the temperature in Fahrenheit

If you click at the button named "Fahrenheit to Celsius", it will ask you to enter the temperature in Fahrenheit and then it will convert it to Celsius and then will display the temperature in Celsius
